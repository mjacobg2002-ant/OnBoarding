## 1. Company Snapshot

**Kind Supply Digital (KSD)** is a boutique growth agency. We build websites and run ongoing digital growth (SEO + paid ads) for local and regional service businesses — primarily in three niches where we've built repeatable expertise: behavioral health, law firms, and home services.

We are not a generalist agency pitching "digital marketing" broadly. Every conversation should sound like it's coming from someone who understands that specific owner's business.

## 5. Internal Process — The Two-Call Model

This is the backbone of everything in this package. Read it twice.

1. **Rep sources and qualifies** a lead (LEAK score) via cold outreach.
2. **Rep books and runs the Discovery Call** — gathers needs, timeline, and basics. **Rep does not quote pricing on this call.**
3. **Rep sends a Quick Summary to Marcel** immediately after discovery (template in `07_Closing_Mechanics_and_Marcel_Handoff.md`).
4. **Marcel sets pricing + pitch direction** based on that summary.
5. **Rep books and runs the Close/Presentation Call** — presents the offer as directed, handles objections, and asks for the close.
6. **Rep collects the deposit and contract signature** (Stripe). Retainer work begins on the signature date.

Two distinct appointment types exist: **Discovery** and **Close**. Daily/weekly appointment targets (below) count both types combined.
