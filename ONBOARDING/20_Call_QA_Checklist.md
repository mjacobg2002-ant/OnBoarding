# Call QA Checklist
### For Marcel/manager use when reviewing a live or recorded call.

---

## QA Cadence Recommendation

- **Weeks 1–4:** review 2–3 calls per rep, per week
- **Month 2+:** review 1–2 calls per rep, per week, plus a monthly deeper pass
- Mix it up: review at least one Discovery call and one Close call per rep per month, not just cold calls

---

## The Checklist

### Opening
- [ ] Used a pattern-interrupt opener (not "how are you today")
- [ ] Gave a specific, credible reason for the call
- [ ] Asked permission before qualifying questions

### Qualifying (LEAK)
- [ ] Covered at least 2 of the 4 LEAK areas
- [ ] Asked open-ended questions, not just yes/no
- [ ] Actually listened to the answer before moving on (didn't just wait to talk)

### Objection Handling
- [ ] Paused before responding (didn't interrupt or jump in defensively)
- [ ] Validated the objection genuinely
- [ ] Reframed back to the prospect's actual pain/goal
- [ ] Advanced with a specific next step or question

### Appointment Setting
- [ ] Asked directly for a specific day/time (not vague)
- [ ] Confirmed the right decision-maker will be on the call
- [ ] Set clear expectations for what happens on the next call

### Compliance / Tone
- [ ] No guaranteed results or outcomes promised
- [ ] No pricing quoted before Marcel's direction (Discovery calls)
- [ ] Professional, respectful tone throughout — no pressure tactics

### Next Steps
- [ ] HubSpot logged same-day (activity, notes, stage update)
- [ ] Calendar invite sent for any booked appointment
- [ ] Marcel Handoff Summary sent same-day (if a Discovery call)

---

## Scoring Guidance

Each section above is pass/fail. **3 or more unchecked boxes in any single call = flag for a 1:1 coaching conversation**, referencing the matching row in `19_Failure_Modes_and_Corrections.md`. This isn't punitive — it's the fastest way to catch a fixable pattern before it becomes a habit.
