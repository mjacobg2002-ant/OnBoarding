# Evaluation Rubrics + Certification

Score every roleplay and, later, every real recorded call, against these rubrics. Consistent scoring is what makes "certified" actually mean something.

---

## Cold Call Rubric

| Dimension | 1 (Weak) | 3 (Solid) | 5 (Excellent) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Opener | Generic, rushed, or apologetic | Clear pattern-interrupt, but slightly rushed | Confident, natural, earns real attention |
| Qualifying (LEAK) | Skips straight to pitching | Asks 1–2 relevant questions | Surfaces real, specific pain using LEAK cleanly |
| Value Bridge | Full pitch instead of a bridge | Reasonable one-liner, a bit generic | Sharp, specific to what prospect just said |
| Ask For Appointment | Vague ("maybe sometime?") | Asks for a time, accepts pushback easily | Direct, assumptive, offers two concrete times |
| Tone/Pace | Sounds scripted or nervous | Generally natural | Fully conversational, controlled pace |

**Score: ___ / 25**

## Discovery Call Rubric

| Dimension | 1 (Weak) | 3 (Solid) | 5 (Excellent) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Rapport + Agenda-Setting | Jumps straight into questions | Sets a basic agenda | Sets a clear agenda, explains what happens next (no pricing today) |
| Discovery Depth (LEAK) | Surface-level, few questions | Covers all 4 LEAK areas | Digs past first answers to real specifics and numbers |
| Timeline/Decision Process | Not asked | Asked, not fully explored | Fully maps who's involved and what's driving urgency |
| Recap + Next-Step Expectations | Vague or missing | Recaps main points | Clear recap + specific expectation of what happens next and when |

**Score: ___ / 20**

---

## Objection Handling Rubric (P.V.R.A.)

| Dimension | 1 (Weak) | 3 (Solid) | 5 (Excellent) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pause | Interrupts or jumps in defensively | Brief pause, occasionally rushes | Consistently lets the objection land fully |
| Validate | Skips or sounds insincere | Validates, sometimes generic | Genuine, specific validation |
| Reframe | Argues or ignores the objection | Reasonable reframe | Sharp reframe tied to prospect's own words |
| Advance | No clear next step offered | Asks a question or proposes a step | Confident, specific next step every time |

**Score: ___ / 20**

---

## Closing Call Rubric

| Dimension | 1 (Weak) | 3 (Solid) | 5 (Excellent) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Recap + Presentation | Jumps straight to price, no recap | Recaps briefly | Ties recommendation directly back to prospect's stated pain |
| Presenting the Investment | Apologetic, trails off | States price clearly | States price with full confidence, no hedging |
| Objection Handling at Close | Freezes or gets defensive | Applies P.V.R.A., a bit shaky | Fully composed, P.V.R.A. applied cleanly |
| Asking For the Close | Doesn't ask directly, waits for prospect | Asks, somewhat softly | Direct, confident ask + clear next action (contract/deposit) |

**Score: ___ / 20**

## Certification Checklist (Go-Live Gate)

A new hire is cleared to go live — dialing real leads solo — only when **all** boxes below are checked:

- [ ] Cold Call Rubric: 4.0+/5 average across at least 3 separate roleplays
- [ ] Discovery Call Rubric: 3.5+/5 average across at least 2 roleplays
- [ ] Objection Handling Rubric: 3.5+/5 average, including at least 1 Advanced-tier scenario
- [ ] Closing Call Rubric: 3.5+/5 average across at least 2 roleplays
- [ ] Successfully completed the **General-Advanced-1 Certification Final Exam** roleplay (`17_Roleplay_Bank_All_Levels.md`)
- [ ] Can write a complete, correctly-formatted Marcel Handoff Summary unprompted
- [ ] Demonstrates working knowledge of HubSpot pipeline stages and required properties
- [ ] Has reviewed `19_Failure_Modes_and_Corrections.md` in full
- [ ] Signed off by Marcel/trainer

**If any box is unchecked:** do not send the rep live. Add targeted roleplay reps on the specific weak area and re-test within 1–2 days, not a full week — momentum matters.
