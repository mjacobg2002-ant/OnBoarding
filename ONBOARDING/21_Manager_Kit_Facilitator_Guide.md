# Manager Kit: Facilitator Guide
### How Marcel runs this training — this time, and every time after.

---

## Before Any New Hire Starts

- [ ] Share this entire folder with the new hire ahead of Day 1
- [ ] Provision HubSpot access (see `16_HubSpot_Pipeline_and_Lead_Tracking.md` for what they'll need)
- [ ] Set up dialer/phone and Stripe access
- [ ] Decide: 3-Day (`12_Onboarding_Agenda_3Day.md`) or 2-Day (`13_Onboarding_Agenda_2Day.md`) track — default to 3-Day unless there's a specific reason to compress
- [ ] Block your own calendar for the full training window — this program only works if the trainer is actually present for roleplays, not multitasking

---

## Running Each Day

- Follow the hour-by-hour agenda in the relevant onboarding file. Don't skip blocks to "save time" — the roleplay blocks in particular are the highest-leverage part of this program. A new hire who reads every script but never runs a live roleplay will freeze on their first real call.
- When playing the prospect in roleplays (`17_Roleplay_Bank_All_Levels.md`), stay in character and actually push back — a trainer who goes easy produces a rep who's unprepared for a real, skeptical owner.
- Use the rubrics in `18_Evaluation_Rubrics_and_Certification.md` for every scored roleplay, even early on. Real-time feedback tied to a consistent rubric builds skill faster than "good job" or "needs work" alone.
- End each day with the debrief block on the agenda — 30 minutes of "what worked, what didn't, what's tomorrow" keeps the new hire oriented.

---

## Grading Guidance

- Be specific in feedback: tie every note back to a rubric dimension ("your Reframe step was strong, but you skipped Pause and jumped straight to defending the price") rather than general impressions.
- It's normal for Day 1 scores to be low across the board — the rubrics aren't a test to pass on Day 1, they're a shared language for coaching all the way through the 30-Day Ramp.
- Don't inflate certification scores to move faster. The Certification Checklist in `18_Evaluation_Rubrics_and_Certification.md` exists specifically so a rep doesn't go live under-prepared — a slower, real certification beats a fast, fake one every time.

## Common Trainer Mistakes To Avoid

| Mistake | Why It Hurts | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Skipping roleplay reps to cover more material faster | Rep can recite frameworks but freezes live | Protect roleplay time as non-negotiable |
| Going easy as the "prospect" in roleplays | Rep is unprepared for real pushback | Stay in character, push back genuinely, especially in Intermediate/Advanced tiers |
| Certifying a rep who hasn't hit rubric thresholds, to hit a start date | Rep goes live shaky, early real calls go poorly, confidence takes a hit | Add a half-day rather than compromise the Go-Live Gate |
| Vague feedback ("that was good") | Rep doesn't know what to repeat or fix | Always tie feedback to a specific rubric dimension |
| Not shadowing/reviewing early live calls | Bad habits set in before anyone notices | Follow the QA cadence in `20_Call_QA_Checklist.md` from Week 1 |

## Go/No-Go Certification Sign-Off

Use the Certification Checklist in `18_Evaluation_Rubrics_and_Certification.md` at the end of Day 3 (3-Day track) or Day 2 (2-Day track). This is a real gate, not a formality — a rep should only go live solo once every box is checked.

## Customizing This Program For Future Hires

- **If a future hire will specialize in one niche** (rather than working all three), compress the other two niche playbook sections in Day 1/2 to a light overview and go deeper on their primary niche instead.
- **If pricing, offer tiers, or the process flow change,** update `02_Company_Offer_Reference.md` first — every other file points back to it, so that's the one place a change needs to happen for the whole package to stay accurate.
- **If a new objection pattern keeps coming up** that isn't in `06_Objection_Handling_Master.md` or the niche playbooks, add it — these documents are meant to grow with real field experience, not stay frozen as written today.
- **Duplicate `22_Manager_Kit_New_Hire_Tracker.md` fresh for every new hire** — don't reuse one rep's tracker for the next person.
