# 2-Day Onboarding Agenda (Compressed)
### Use when speed matters more than depth — e.g., a hire who needs to start dialing fast, or a rep with prior sales experience. The 3-Day track (`12_Onboarding_Agenda_3Day.md`) is the recommended default.

**What's different from the 3-Day track:** niche playbook depth is compressed to one focused pass instead of a full deep-dive per niche, and roleplay volume is reduced. To compensate, **Week 1 of the 30-Day Ramp Plan for 2-Day-track hires gets extra roleplay reps built in** (see `14_30Day_Ramp_Plan.md`) — the depth isn't skipped, it's shifted into the first week of live work.
